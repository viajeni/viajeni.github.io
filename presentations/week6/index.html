Wireframing Week 6

Concept

We are a new local restaurant who is looking for a website. Be creative with designing the wireframe. We want our site to be user friendly and you must include a navigation bar, header, sidebar, content (H2 heading and body text), and a mimimum of three images.

Assignment Instructions

Part One

  • Sketch out a wireframe of what you think the website should look like on paper
  • Discuss with the people next to you and decide what elements are best from each person's wireframe

Part Two

  • Create a wireframe using Photoshop OR Illustrator which ever you are most comfortable with.
  • Create a Desktop and Mobile (Responsive) Layout
  • Upload it onto your own repo
  • Include a breif description of what creative elements you would use (fonts, colours, potential pictures, backgrounds etc.) if you were to turn this wireframe into a mockup ON UPLOAD.HTML

Upload Instructions

When you are satisfied with what you have created...

  1. Save your Photoshop OR Illustrator file as PNG
  2. Add your PNG file to your images folder
  3. Click on Week 6 "upload.html" from Bianca's Repo
  4. Copy the "RAW" code and paste it into your Week 6 Presentation Folder
  5. Insert your first name in the "h1" tag
  6. Add both of the PNG files in the appropriate IMG tag
  7. ** DON'T FORGET TO INSERT A BREIF DESCRIPTION OF POTENTIAL CREATIVE ELEMENTS IN A COMMENT ON THE UPLAOD.HTML**
  8. Save the file, commit to master, and push to origin
