Mind Mapping

Instructions

Create an account on the website Coggle and create a mind map that you will later turn into a mockup for a website. This website will be an online presentation of your own portfolio.

Make sure to include elements such as colours, different fonts, images, and a theme that represents you and your portfolio.

Once the mindmap is created, go into Adobe Illustrator and create a mockup of your portfolio website. Create it on an 8 x 11 landscape template.

When Finished

Download your mind map from Coggle by using the download button and then pressing image, PNG file. You can then place in the upload.html that you can get from Maddie's Repo. Place a screenshot of your mindmap from Coggle into the upload.html and add your name to the header of the page.

After both are placed in upload.html save everything, commit and push to origin.

Rubric

Colours: (Do your colours match your overall theme?) 0/2

Font: (Do your font choices add to the colours and theme? If used more than one font, do the different fonts compliment each other? Are the fonts legible?) 0/3

Theme: (Is there a clear overall theme?) 0/2

Mind Map: (Were the elements stated in the mindmap used in the mockup? Was the mind map organized and easy to read? Did the mind map demonstrate brainstorming for the mockup?) 0/5

Does the mockup represent a portfolio? 0/2

Total: 0/15
